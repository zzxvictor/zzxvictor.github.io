Frequently Asked Questions

What makes the House of Dreams special?
  • We prioritize ethical education. If you're looking for a coach to plagiarize and write your essay for you, we're not who you're looking for. At the House of Dreams, we believe that education can only be effective when it's ethical. Therefore, we inspire critical thinking and teach writing skills. Students take away valuable writing skills from our essay guidance processes.
  • We’ve got the best essay coaches. The personal statement and supplemental essays are the most important parts of an application. Instead of using mundane one-for-all essay templates, we inspire students to write memorable personal statements that reveal their individualities.
  • We’ve got the experts of each field.All our coaches have been through the application processes and were successfully admitted to top universities. We will also be able to offer valuable industry advice since we have worked in large companies such as Deloitte, PwC, Ogilvy, and Twitter.

Unlike other agencies where there could be different coaches assigned to different parts of the essay writing process, we assign one coach to manage your entire process, from brainstorming ideas to polishing language. This will ensure that the essay turns out to be one piece of consistent writing.
I’m currently in high school. When is the best time to start preparing?

Admission officers consider your grades and extracurriculars from grades 9-12. The application deadlines for American or Canadian universities are usually somewhere between December to the start of the next calendar year. Therefore, we strongly encourage students to come to us during the summer between grade 11-12 so that we get to thoroughly know the student and start brainstorming for the personal statement.

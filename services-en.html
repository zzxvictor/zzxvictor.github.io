Our Packages

Applying to universities and graduate schools may be more complicated and time-consuming than you think. It involves much more than simply submitting your grades and test scores. Therefore, it is highly recommended that you start preparing two years before the time of application. Our coaches will discover your virtues and activity highlights, and present you as a holistic applicant to college admission officers.

We provide the following services for applicants to U.S., Canada, Australia, and the U.K.

Studio Pack All-Inclusive Studio Pack

The All-Inclusive Studio Pack
Details

This all-inclusive package includes everything you need for a successful application, including:

  1. Choosing programs to apply to according to the student’s background
  2. Creating a timeline and choosing ED/EA/RD schools
  3. Common Application resume editing, including activities from grades 9-12
  4. Personal Statement guidance and editing, including at least three proofreads
  5. Providing advice on recommendation letters
  6. Interview training and mock interviews for every program
  7. Expert advice from experts in the field you want to go into
  8. Choosing which offer to take and college prep
